Signature Keynotes

Each keynote is customizable to your audience and event format.

01

“Stop Auditioning for Belonging”

Your most talented people are spending more energy fitting in than leading, and your organization is paying for it in turnover, disengagement, and lost innovation. This talk names the cost and what to do about it.

Best for: Leadership summits, HR conferences, DEIA events

02

“When the C-Suite No Longer Fits”

What happens when your most successful leaders hit the top and realize the system they mastered was never designed for them? A talk about alignment, ambition, and knowing when to rebuild.

Best for: Women in leadership events, executive retreats

03

“Under Pressure, You Don’t Rise. You Fall Back.”

Based on the BOLD™ Method. The leadership habits you build in calm are the ones you fall back on in crisis. This talk gives your audience the framework to build better habits.

Best for: Leadership development programs, management conferences

04

“Upholding Boundaries Is a Business Strategy”

Boundaries aren’t about saying no. They’re about protecting your leaders’ capacity to say yes to what matters. A talk that reframes boundaries as a strategic leadership practice.

Best for: HR conferences, women in leadership, burnout and wellness panels

05

“Black Skin, Bold Heart”: The Book Talk

An unflinching talk from the memoir, for anyone who’s wondered if the system is worth the price of admission.

Best for: DEIA conferences, keynote dinners, author series, leadership retreats
