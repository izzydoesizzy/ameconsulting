Keynotes & Workshops That Shift How Leaders Think

Aileen M. Edwards brings the BOLD™ Method, the raw honesty of her memoir, and the kind of executive experience that can only come from sitting in the chair — to every stage.

Aileen M. Edwards, MA, ACC, RCC is a leadership consultant, executive coach, workplace investigator, and author of Black Skin, Bold Heart. A former CHRO and Senior Director at some of Canada’s largest healthcare organizations, she speaks from the intersection of executive experience, racial identity, and organizational transformation. Her keynotes challenge audiences to rethink what leadership looks like — and who gets to practice it.

Signature Keynotes

Each keynote is customizable to your audience and event format.

01

“Stop Auditioning for Belonging”

Your most talented people are spending more energy fitting in than leading. This keynote names the cost — and gives your organization a way to stop paying it.

02

“When the C-Suite No Longer Fits”

What happens when you achieve everything you were supposed to want — and realize it doesn’t fit? A candid talk about the courage it takes to leave a career you’ve outgrown. Drawn from Aileen’s own journey from CHRO to consultant.

03

“Under Pressure, You Don’t Rise — You Fall Back”

The moment everything goes wrong is the moment your leadership is actually tested. This keynote introduces a framework for building the reflexes that hold up when your plan doesn’t.

04

“Upholding Boundaries Is a Business Strategy”

Your highest performers aren’t burning out from the work — they’re burning out from the lack of limits. This talk reframes boundaries from self-care language into strategic leadership practice with measurable organizational impact.

05

“Black Skin, Bold Heart” — The Book Talk

What does it cost to build a career in spaces that weren’t built for you? An intimate, unflinching talk drawn from Aileen’s memoir — for anyone who’s ever wondered whether the system they’re navigating is worth the price of admission.
