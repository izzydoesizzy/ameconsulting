Leadership Development Programs

Multi-phase programs that transform how your leadership team communicates, collaborates, and leads under pressure.

What It Is

AME Consulting designs and delivers multi-phase leadership development programs for organizational teams. These aren’t one-off workshops — they’re sustained engagements that build leadership capacity from the inside out, integrating assessment, learning, coaching, and accountability over weeks or months.

What’s Included

CliftonStrengths Mapping

Individual and team strengths assessments to identify natural talent patterns and blind spots

360 Assessments

Multi-rater feedback to build self-awareness and identify development priorities

Emotional Intelligence

Workshops focused on self-regulation, empathy, and social awareness in high-stakes environments

Strategic Conversations

Crucial Conversations methodology for navigating difficult dialogue with confidence and clarity

Individual Coaching

1:1 coaching sessions for leaders who need personalized support alongside group work

Follow-Up & Accountability

Structured follow-up to ensure insights become lasting behavioral change

Who It’s For

Leadership development programs are designed for organizations with 10–100+ leaders who are navigating:

  • ◆ Culture shifts or post-merger integration
  • ◆ Accountability and trust breakdowns across teams
  • ◆ New leadership appointments needing rapid development
  • ◆ Succession planning and emerging leader pipelines
  • ◆ Multi-site coordination challenges

The Process

1

Discovery

Deep-dive into organizational context, challenges, and leadership landscape

2

Assessment

CliftonStrengths, 360s, and stakeholder interviews to build a clear picture

3

Program Design

Custom curriculum tailored to your organization’s specific leadership gaps and goals

4

Delivery

Multi-phase workshops, team sessions, and individual coaching over weeks or months

5

Follow-Up Coaching

Sustained support to embed new practices and measure progress

Case Study Outline

Regional Children’s Services Organization

Challenge

A regional children’s services organization was experiencing accountability breakdowns, trust deficits, and communication challenges across multiple sites and leadership levels.

Approach

AME Consulting designed a multi-phase leadership development program incorporating CliftonStrengths mapping, 360 feedback, Crucial Conversations training, and individual coaching for senior leaders.

Outcome

Measurable improvements in cross-site collaboration, team trust scores, and leadership confidence. The organization reported stronger alignment between leadership behavior and organizational values.
