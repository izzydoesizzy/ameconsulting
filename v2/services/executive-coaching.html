Executive Coaching

1:1 coaching using the proprietary BOLD™ Method for leaders navigating executive presence, high-stakes conversations, and career transitions.

Who It’s For

Executive coaching at AME Consulting is designed for women leaders — particularly women of color — in mid-to-senior roles who are navigating the complex dynamics of authority, identity, and belonging in corporate and organizational environments.

You might be preparing for a bigger role, managing a difficult organizational dynamic, questioning whether the path you’re on still fits, or simply needing a trusted thinking partner who understands the terrain from lived experience.

“I coach the woman who has the credentials, the track record, and the results — but something keeps her from fully stepping into her power.”

The BOLD™ Method Framework

Every coaching engagement is built on the BOLD™ Method — a framework that moves leaders through four stages of development:

B

Becoming

Shedding the stories that teach you to stay small

O

Owning

Claiming authority and worth without apology

L

Leading

Influencing under pressure and building trust

D

Disrupting

Redesigning what leadership looks like
