Workplace Investigations

Independent, impartial third-party investigations that protect your organization and uphold employee rights.

Scope

AME Consulting provides independent third-party workplace investigations in accordance with Ontario’s legal framework. These investigations are conducted with the rigor, impartiality, and thoroughness required to protect both the organization and the individuals involved.

Ontario Bill 168

Violence & harassment in the workplace

Human Rights Code

Discrimination & harassment complaints

OHSA

Occupational Health & Safety Act obligations

The Process

1

Intake & Scoping

Review the complaint, determine scope, establish terms of reference, and define the investigation plan

2

Complainant Interview

Detailed interview with the complainant to establish the full scope of allegations and identify witnesses

3

Respondent Interview

Fair and thorough interview with the respondent, ensuring their right to be heard

4

Witness Interviews

Typically 5–15 witness interviews, depending on the scope and complexity of the complaint

5

Documentation Review

Review of relevant emails, policies, performance records, and any other documentary evidence

6

Credibility Assessment

Rigorous analysis of all testimony and evidence using established credibility assessment frameworks

7

Comprehensive Report

Detailed findings of fact, credibility analysis, and recommendations delivered in a report that withstands arbitration scrutiny

Qualifications

Aileen brings unique qualifications to workplace investigations that most external investigators simply don’t have:

  • ◆ 30+ years of progressive HR leadership including enterprise-level labor relations
  • ◆ Former CHRO at Markham Stouffville Hospital — she understands investigations from the employer side
  • ◆ Deep understanding of the human dynamics that drive workplace conflict, not just the legal framework
  • ◆ Cultural competence that ensures all parties feel heard and respected throughout the process
  • ◆ Reports that withstand arbitration — detailed, methodical, and built on sound investigative principles

Quality Standard

Every investigation report produced by AME Consulting is built to withstand arbitration scrutiny. Our methodology follows established best practices for workplace investigations in Ontario, ensuring procedural fairness, thorough evidence gathering, and defensible findings of fact.
