30 Years in the System. Then She Changed It.

She knows what it costs to lead where you weren’t expected to — because she paid it.

The Rise

Labor negotiations at 2 AM. Leadership programs built from nothing. Executives coached through mergers that put 500 jobs on the line. From HR Director to CHRO to Senior Director at Unity Health Toronto.

The Reckoning

The higher she climbed, the smaller the room got. A Black woman in Canadian healthcare leadership, navigating an environment that welcomed her contributions but questioned her presence. Until achievement and reckoning collided.

The Rebuilding

She didn’t leave because she failed. She left because she succeeded — and built AME Consulting to be the strategist and truth-teller she wished she’d had.
